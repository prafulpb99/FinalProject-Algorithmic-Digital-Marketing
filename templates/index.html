Ecommerce Marketing Analytics
Understanding the customers well is the key to any successful business. It's a good practise to store their demographic information and their preferences and to provide customers with a personalized and an enjoyable experience on the platform. To understand customers well, we need to analyse their purchase history and behaviour. By collecting and analysing historic data we can predict what kind of offers would excite them and at the same time what would bring more revenue to the business.
